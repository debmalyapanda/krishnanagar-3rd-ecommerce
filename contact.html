CLIENT SERVICE FAQS

What does White-Glove Shipping & Assembly entail?

Our dedicated white-glove transport team safely carries your luxury furniture into your room of choice, unpacks each marble slab or sofa, performs complete assembly, and removes all protective packaging.

Can I request custom stone or velvet finish options?

Yes, AURA Atelier offers bespoke customization. We can source specific Nero Marquina or Calacatta marble slabs and custom dye velvet and raw silk drapery to complement your architectural swatches.

What is the warranty coverage on solid wood frames?

All solid American Walnut and Japanese Oak structural frames carry a 10-Year Lifetime Structural Guarantee against joint failure or warping.
